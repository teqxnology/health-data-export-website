About Health Data Export

Health Data Export is the leading health and fitness data export solution for Android and iOS. Our app enables users to export their health data from Apple Health, Google Fit, Health Connect, and all major wearables including Fitbit, Garmin, Samsung Health, and more.

Key Features

  • Full Health Connect API integration
  • Support for 35+ health and fitness platforms
  • Export to Google Sheets and Excel CSV
  • 100% local processing - complete privacy
  • Works with all major wearables and fitness trackers
  • Simple intuitive interface

Statistics

  • 100K+ million data points exported
  • 4/5 average rating
  • Available on Android (iOS coming soon)
