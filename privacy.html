Privacy Policy – Health Data Export

Effective date: 2026-02-20

Developer: Teqxnology (“we”, “us”, “our”)

Contact: teqxnology@gmail.com

Health Data Export helps you export your health information from Health Connect to your Google Sheets account and/or to CSV files on your device.

1) Information We Collect

a) Health data (via Health Connect)

If you grant permission, the app can read health data from Health Connect, such as:

  • Activity (e.g., steps, distance, calories, exercise sessions)
  • Body measurements (e.g., weight, body fat, height)
  • Sleep information
  • Vitals (e.g., heart rate, blood pressure, blood glucose)
  • Nutrition and cycle tracking data

We do not collect or receive this health data on our own servers. Health data is processed on your device and exported only to destinations you choose.

b) Google account information

If you connect Google, the app uses Google Sign-In to access your account for exporting data to Google Sheets. The app may access:

  • Your selected Google account email address
  • Permission to create/update spreadsheets you select

c) App settings and preferences (stored locally)

The app stores certain settings on your device, including selected data types, Spreadsheet IDs, export frequency, and purchase status. These are stored locally on your device.

d) Billing information (Premium feature)

Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your payment card information; Google only shares limited purchase status to enable features.

2) How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Read health data from Health Connect (with permission)
  • Export data to your Google Sheet or CSV files
  • Display export status and enable premium features
  • Improve app stability and error reporting

3) Data Sharing and Disclosure

a) Google Sheets: Data is sent to the spreadsheet you specify, subject to Google’s privacy policy.

b) No sale of personal data: We do not sell your personal or health data.

c) No third-party analytics: We do not share health data with third-party analytics or advertising networks.

4) Data Storage and Retention

  • Health data is processed on-device and exported to your chosen destination.
  • Settings remain on your device until the app is uninstalled or data is cleared.
  • Exported data remains in your control within Google Sheets or CSV files.

5) Your Choices and Controls

You can revoke Health Connect permissions, disconnect your Google account, delete exported files, or uninstall the app at any time.

6) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and Google account.

7) Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will update the “Effective date” and provide notice within the app or store listing.

9) Contact Us

Questions? Contact us at: teqxnology@gmail.com
