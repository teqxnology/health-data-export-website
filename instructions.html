Instructions & FAQ

Everything you need to get started with Health Data Export

Quick Start Guide

1

Install the App

Download Health Data Export from the App Store or Google Play. Connect to your Google Sheets.

2

Grant Permissions

Grant necessary permissions in Health Connect to access your health data.

3

Select Data to Export

Choose which health metrics you want to export: steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts, nutrition, and more. Use date filters to narrow your selection.

4

Choose Export Option

Select your preferred export option: Google Sheets or CSV.

5

Export & Analyze

Tap the export button and choose where to save your file. Your data is ready to analyze, visualize, or share with healthcare providers.
